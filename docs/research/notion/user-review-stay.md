# Notion: Why Users Stay

Based on G2 (10,149 verified reviews, 4.6 stars), Capterra, Reddit, and other sources (2024-2026).

## 1. Customization & Flexibility
- "Set it up in a way that makes sense for you"
- "Clean and minimal interface" that's "flexible yet has some structure"
- Users love it for school, personal goals, professional projects
- "Helps fit their individual way of thinking"

## 2. All-in-One Workspace
- "The 'all-in-one' workspace that actually lives up to the name"
- Notes, wikis, databases, project management in one platform
- "Pretty much anything — whether it's a database, a log, or even a simple note"
- "Changed the way I store information"

## 3. Replacing Scattered Tools
- "The tool that finally helped them replace scattered docs, spreadsheets, and project trackers"
- One central place instead of information in Slack threads, scattered docs, multiple apps
- Consolidation is a major pull factor

## 4. Visual Project Tracking
- "Visual roadmap of project progress"
- Switch from overview to specific pages seamlessly
- 6 database views (Table, Board, Timeline, Calendar, Gallery, List) for different perspectives

## 5. It Grows With You
- "Once adopted, it rarely stays a single-purpose tool"
- Evolves from documentation hub into connected planning/collaboration workspace
- Expandable without rebuilding core systems

## 6. Documentation & Knowledge Management
- "Feels irreplaceable" for documentation and notes
- Single source of truth for project docs to personal notes
- Easy linking between everything
- Wiki verification builds trust in content accuracy

## 7. Templates & Community
- Thousands of templates for every use case
- Active template marketplace and community
- Quick start without building from scratch

## 8. AI Features (2025)
- "Context-aware AI becoming a massive productivity booster"
- AI Q&A for instant workspace knowledge retrieval
- AI-generated formulas, summaries, translations
- Notion 3.0 Agents for autonomous workflow automation

## 9. Aesthetics & Design
- Clean, polished interface with custom icons and covers
- Visually satisfying workspace that users enjoy using
- Good design drives daily engagement

## 10. Cross-Platform
- Web, desktop (Windows, macOS), mobile (iOS, Android)
- Offline support (with sync when connected)
- Consistent experience across devices

## Key Retention Insight
Notion's retention is driven by **investment and lock-in through customization**. Once teams build their workflows, databases, and knowledge bases in Notion, the switching cost becomes enormous — not because the data is locked in, but because the *structure and workflows* are. The more you build, the harder it is to replicate elsewhere. This is a double-edged sword: it keeps users, but frustrates those who feel trapped.
